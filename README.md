🚀 About the Game
Classic snake reimagined in modern style! Guide the snake to eat food, grow as long as possible, and survive without colliding with walls or your own body. Enjoy smooth animations, wavy backgrounds, and responsive controls for both desktop and mobile.

Smooth segment-based snake movement

Food with glowing pulse animation

Wavy animated background with modern graphics

Multiple background music themes in WAV format

Sound effects for food pickup and collisions

Difficulty increases automatically as you grow

Fully responsive for phones and desktop

Dark and light mode support

Local leaderboard to track your top scores

📖 How to Play
Use arrow keys on desktop, or swipe on mobile to control the snake

Eat the food to grow

Each food increases your score

Avoid crashing into walls or yourself

Survive as long as possible and aim for a high score
